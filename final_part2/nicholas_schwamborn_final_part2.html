Course Review Questions

What was your previous knowledge of HTML/CSS/Javascript?

Prior to this course, I did not have any knowledge of HTML, CSS, or Javascript, but I did know java which made it easier to understand formatting and the process.

Did you use a template or did you build your portfolio from scratch?

I used the template provided for my portfolio, but I changed the key details of it such as the background and text design to add more originality to it.

What was your most challenging part of your portfolio (ie, the template, artifacts, CSS, etc.)

Honestly, the hardest part of the portfolio was making sure the images of the artifacts are correctly spaced and lined up with their descriptions.

How taking this class changed your view on Web Development?

This class made me realize how much effort and knowledge is truly needed behind the scenes to create a well-made and functionable website.

What would you recommend to future students taking this class?

This is definitely a class I would recommend for students to take to improve their general knowledge on HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
